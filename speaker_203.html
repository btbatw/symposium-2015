Li-San Wang, Ph.D.
王立三 博士

Associate Professor, Bio info. Pathology, University of Pennsylvania

Li-San Wang received his B.S. and M.S. in Electrical Engineering from the National Taiwan University, and his Ph.D. in Computer Sciences from the University of Texas at Austin. Currently he is an Associate Professor of Pathology and Laboratory Medicine and Chair of the Genomics and Computational Biology graduate program at the University of Pennsylvania Perelman School of Medicine. Dr. Wang's research integrates bioinformatics, genomics, and human genetics in the study of Alzheimer’s disease and neurodegenerative disorders. He is the principle investigator of the NIA Genetics of Alzheimer’s Disease Data Storage Site (NIAGADS), a data repository supported by the National Institute of Aging for Alzheimer’s disease genetics research.
